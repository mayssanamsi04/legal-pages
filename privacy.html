Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

MarketFlow AI ("we," "our," or "us") operates the web application located at artificial-insight.xyz (the "Platform"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use MarketFlow AI, including when you connect your TikTok account via TikTok for Developers APIs.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in using TikTok Login Kit, we receive your TikTok Open ID, display name, and profile avatar via the user.info.basic scope.
  • Content Data: When you use the content scheduling feature, we temporarily store video files and associated metadata (caption, hashtags, publish time) using the video.upload and video.publish scopes until the post is published or deleted.
  • Analytics Data: We retrieve your published TikTok video list and engagement metrics via the video.list scope (Display API) to display performance analytics inside your dashboard.
  • Usage Data: We automatically collect standard log data such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps to maintain platform security and improve performance.
  • Communications: If you contact us via email or a support form, we retain your message and contact details to respond to your inquiry.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your identity and connect your TikTok account to MarketFlow AI
  • To display your TikTok profile information within your dashboard
  • To schedule and publish content to your TikTok account on your behalf
  • To retrieve and display your TikTok video library and performance analytics
  • To maintain platform security, prevent fraud, and debug technical issues
  • To respond to support requests and communications
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the features you explicitly use.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party. We may share limited data only in the following circumstances:

  • TikTok: Data is transmitted to and from TikTok's servers through their official APIs as part of normal platform operation.
  • Hosting and Infrastructure Providers: Our platform is hosted on secure cloud infrastructure. Providers only process data on our behalf and under strict data processing agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users or the public.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide the service:

  • Video files uploaded for scheduling are deleted from our servers immediately after successful publishing or upon cancellation of the scheduled post.
  • TikTok analytics data fetched via the Display API is cached temporarily to improve dashboard load times and is refreshed regularly.
  • Account information is retained for as long as your account remains active. Upon account deletion, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days.

5. Your Rights and Controls

You have full control over your data at all times:

  • Disconnect TikTok: You can revoke MarketFlow AI's access to your TikTok account at any time from your TikTok app under Settings → Security → Authorized Apps, or from within MarketFlow AI's account settings page.
  • Delete Your Account: You may request full deletion of your MarketFlow AI account and all associated data by contacting us at the email address below.
  • Access and Correction: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you or ask us to correct any inaccuracies.
  • Data Portability: Upon request, we will provide your data in a commonly used, machine-readable format.

6. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. All data transmitted between your browser and our platform is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. TikTok access tokens are stored securely and are never exposed to the client side or shared with any third party.

7. Children's Privacy

MarketFlow AI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the platform after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
